phonetic inventory

vépárpup has 10 consonants and 10 vowels

v(v), p(p), r(ɾ or ɹ)*, n(n), ñ(ɲ), h(h), j(ç), w(w or ʋ)*, '(ʔ) and ř(ɽ͡r̥).
