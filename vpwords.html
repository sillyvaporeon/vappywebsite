Eeveelutions and their types

The names of eeveelutions in Vépárpup are based simply on their type followed by the suffix -ion, so the words for certain things are identical or at least very similar to the beginning of a certain eeveelution's name.

Eevee - Ivi (/ivi/)

Vaporeon - Vépárion (/ve:pɔ:ɾiɑn/)
Water / Vapor / A body of water / Liquid - Vépár (/ve:pɔ:ɾ/)

Jolteon - Jów'ion (/ço:wʔiɑn/)
Electricity - Jów (/ço:w/)

Flareon - Vrérion (/vɾe:ɾiɑn/)
Fire - Vřé' (/vɽ͡r̥e:ʔ/)

Espeon - Evpion (/ɛvpiɑn/)
Mind - Evpe (/ɛvpɛ/)

Umbreon - Unpřion (/ənpɽ͡r̥iɑn/)
Darkness - Unpařa (/ənpaɽ͡r̥a/)
Dark - Unpeřé (/ənpɛɽ͡r̥e:/)

Leafeon - Rivion (/ɾiviɑn/)
Leaf - Riv (/ɾiv/)
Plant - Rív (/ɾø:v/)

Glaceon - Ñrévion (/ɲɾe:viɑn/)
Ice - Ñrévépop (/ɲɾe:ve:pɑp/)
Cold - Ñréj (/ɲɾe:ç/)

Sylveon - Jíwvion (/çø:wviɑn/)
Fairy - Jíwi (/çø:wi/)
